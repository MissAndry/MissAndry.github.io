New Blog Layout

I want a website with:

  • the navigation bar at the same level as the header, like the "Women in Computing" website, and I want at least some of those links to "jump" to different parts of the page.
  • a center column for most of the content
  • responsivity - the header and nav bar centered at around 600px and no further apart than the content section at around 1200px
  • picture links that respond to a small screen by turning into an easier-to-see column of banners
  • different fonts! not, like, a ton, but a few good ones from Google Fonts instead of just 'Helvetica', 'Arial', 'Times'
