proper burrito construction

  1. Heat tortilla of choice. Place heated tortilla open-face on whatever counter space is available.
  2. Thickly slather some guacamole on there. Guac is an important first ingredient since it will hold everything else together.
  3. Rice, if you want rice (you want rice. It's delicious and more importantly will soak up all the extra meat and bean juices).
  4. Meat, if you're a meat eater.
  5. Cheese, if you're a cheese eater. The cheese should melt into the meat and/or rice to give you all its gooey, greasy goodness.
  6. Beans. These are delicious and will help melt the cheese further.
  7. Salsa, if you're into that.
  8. Fold that shizz up and devour
