First challenge

The site I chose was comiXology.com because they have an interactive, image-heavy interface. The site solves the problem of keeping track of individual single-issues by offering digital copies hosted on the site. Even buying single-issues online is almost unheard of, so if you can't get to the comic book store or miss a printing, chances are comiXology will have what you're looking for.

Some of the visual challenges with this site include the scrolling header, the comics scroll bars, and placement of the quicklinks - the main questions are: How many new comics can we display without being overwhelming?, How can we inform people of new products in an interesting way?, and How can we display individual comics in a way that is useful and navigable?
